About Us





HEALTH IS WEALTH Limited was established in 2021 as a professional Land Surveying, Mapping and Land Development practice. With over ten years' professional experience, we have been involved with various aspects of cadastral surveying, including beacon relocations, subdivisions, consolidations, servitude surveys and estate establishment. Due to changing land surveying developments, and the growing needs of our valued clients, it became necessary to broaden our service offering so as to offer a more multi-disciplinary surveying service under one banner. To this end, we added Town Planning, Drone Mapping, Pipeline Engineering and Road Engineering Surveying, to our list of offered services.









MISSION AND VISION









MISSION

Our mission at HEALTH IS WEALTH Limited as land surveyors and sectional title consultants is to offer excellent service in each of our professional avenues, in accordance with statutory practices, codes of conduct and general business integrity. This allows us to develop our team.

VISION

To be among the leading one-stop Surveying, Geomatic Service and related engineering solutions providing companies in Africa and beyond.

Our core services deal with Cadastral Surveys, Sectional Title Surveys, Engineering and Monitoring Surveys and Drone Mapping, employing the latest technology, systems and procedures. We continually work towards becoming a complete multi-disciplinary practice and we see teamwork on every project we undertake as the key element to success. Our project managers are duty-bound to ensure efficient and regular communication with stakeholders at every step of the undertaking. HEALTH IS WEALTH Limited is equipped with the latest technologies and has the necessary staff and resources to ensure a world-class level of professionalism and service delivery on every job we perform. Our vision is to grow in order to offer a broad spectrum of services, rendering us our clients’ preferred professional service provider.
